ZENITHMARGINS

Zenithmargins was founded in 2013, while Bitcoin was still in its infancy. We saw the potential for bitcoin to revolutionize the financial industry, making trading cryptocurrency faster, more secure, and less expensive on a global scale.
We started Zenithmargins because we wanted to make it easy for businesses and individuals to trade bitcoin and other alt coins. We are currently the largest bitcoin trading platform processor in the world, serving industry-leading merchants and individuals on six continents. We've created a seamless, secure bitcoin trading experience used daily by hundreds of thousands of bitcoin users.
Trade processing was our first contribution to the Bitcoin ecosystem, but it is not our last. With zenithmargins, we're building an open source platform to power the next applications of Bitcoin.
The Zenithmargins secure bitcoin wallet is giving consumers a powerful toolkit for getting started right with bitcoin payments, and the Zenith Card gives bitcoin users a fast way to convert bitcoin into dollars and spend their funds anywhere Visa® is accepted.
Bitcoin's future looks very bright, and we plan on remaining on the forefront of this technology, creating more tools and services for everyone to use in innovative new ways.
